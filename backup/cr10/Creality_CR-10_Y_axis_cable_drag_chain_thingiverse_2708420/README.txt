# Summary

New Combo YZ bracket when using this and https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2757810

Drag chain assembly for CR-10 to avoid Y axis bed cabling from hanging and rubbing across back frame of printer.

Requires 2x t-bolt hammer slot nuts + bolts to connect to frame + zip ties to hold cable to bed bracket and side bracket.

Print:
8x bottom links (100% infill)
8x top links (100% infill)
1x side bracket (>20% infill)
1x bed end bracket (>20% infill)
